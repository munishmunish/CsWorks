ask.Uwindsor


Uwindsor Turk (University of Windsor Turk) is a crowdsourcing platform that makes it easier for individual computer science student and business to outsource their processes and jobs to distribute workforce who can do their work virtually, including anything from collecting projects validation and researches to more tasks, such as library management system, grocery stores system and more.

Uwindsor Turk allows individuals and companies to harness the collective intelligence, skills, and ideas from all computer science students in University of Windsor. It is convenient for our clients and students to accelerate the software development process. 


Client | What's next?

Get Started.

- Register as one of our clients

- Create your first project

- View and edit all your existing projects

What's next?

- Submit your project to our administrator for approval

- Wait until your project is approved

- Post your project to public (our developers)

Choose developers!

- Wait for new applicants

- Choose one from the applicants for a project

- Claim the finished project and approve it


Developer | What's next?

Get Started.

- Register as one of our developers (students with majors under Department of Computer Science only)

- View all posted projects

- Apply for one or more projects that you want to accomplish

What's next?

- Wait for clients to approve your applications

- Start working on your projects once approved

- Contact clients by using message box for any questions regrading to the projects

Nicely Done!

- Submit your finished projects to clients

- Wait clients' approval of your work

- Claim your rewards
